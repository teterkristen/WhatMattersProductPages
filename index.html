Introducing What Matters

What Matters is your one stop shop for your donation needs!
Find the organization you want to give to, make a donation and
track all of the donations you have made.

Download Now

Features

  • Feature 1: Discover new organizations you want to give to.
  • Feature 2: Donate to the organization with just a few pushes of a button.
  • Feature 3: See your full donation history and email it to yourself for your tax records.
  • Feature 4: Set up monthly donations and manage your payment methods.
